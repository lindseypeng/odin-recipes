Fried Rice

Description

Easy and delicious 20 Minute Taiwanese Beef Shacha Fried Rice perfect for any weeknight dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1-1.5 lb of Thinly Sliced Beef
  • 3 cups of Cooked Rice
  • 11 cup of Corns, Peas, and Carrots
  • 2 tbsp of Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Turn the heat up to medium-high. In a pan, drizzle in oil, and once the pan is hot, add in chopped onions. Saute for 2-3 minutes or until translucent
  2. Add in minced garlic and thinly sliced beef and saute together for another 2-3minutes.
